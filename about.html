About Me



My Name Is Kevaughn Simms. I am the page deigner. I currently attend the Montego Bay Community College/University of Technology, Jamaica pursuing an Associate's and Bachelor's Degree in Modular Computer Studies. My hobbies include Graphic Designing, listening to and playing music, surfing the web and drawing. I received this project from my Website Development Lecturer. It was really fun and exciting for me to know that this would be my first official Website. Upon creating this site, I encountered several issues and, at times I felt like giving up but that did not prevent me from completeing this assignment.I have developed a love for this course because my lecturer inspired and encouraged me to excel and do my best. My final words are: 'you can do anything if you put your mind to it'.
